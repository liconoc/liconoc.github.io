Lidia Contreras-Ochando

PhD Student in Computer Science - Universitat Politècnica de València

Hi, I'm Lidia!

I'm a PhD Student in Computer Science in the DMIP team (a subgroup of ELP) of the Department of Computer Systems and Computation of the Universitat Politènica de València, under the supervision of José Hernández-Orallo and Cèsar Ferri.

Data science is essential for analysing the huge amount of data generated from many different sources. However, the most tedious task of the process, data wrangling, still resists automation partly because the problem strongly depends on domain information that becomes a bottleneck for the systems as the diversity of domains, formats and structures of the data increases. My research is focus on generating algorithms to dynamically handle the background knowledge for the automation of the data wrangling process.

I'm supported by a MECD FPU Grant (2016-2020): REF FPU15/03219 from the Spanish Government.

In the last two years I've been two times in KU Leuven as visiting scholar at DTAI Group under the supervision of Luc De Raedt. Previous to the PhD, I worked as a Researcher in the Universitat Politènica de València (2015-2016) and in the Université de Strasbourg (2016), under the supervision of Nicolas Lachiche, for the REFRAME project. In 2016, I received my M.Sc. from the Universitat Politènica de València with first class honours. I'm also hold a Bacherlor's Degree in Computer Science and I'm Technical Engineer in Data Processing.
